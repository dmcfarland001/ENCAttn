    First and foremost, there was a degree of meta thinking that went into creating this page. Particularly because the topic had to deal with attention and distraction. Despite being knee deep in the research behind why it happens and how to fix it, it was still prevalent in day to day life. In this sense, the lesson was that this topic is both important and applicable to the students that will be visiting our page. This page will serve as one of the foundational resources for the course. It helps set the basis for understanding online behavior and why it has a hold on us. Being that this is a course all about digital environments we feel that the cause, effect and solution to distraction are the perfect way to introduce the content the class will uncover.
